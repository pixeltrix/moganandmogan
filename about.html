About Us

Michael Mogan

Michael has more than 21 years of experience of fundraising at the highest level. Michael raised millions for Coventry Cathedral during his 11 year tenure and has also raised significant sums for music, health, community, welfare and sport charities.

Testimonials from the current Dean, former Provost and the Chairman have kindly been provided for this site.

Diane Kaczmar

Diane has over 18 years experience of working in the voluntary sector for both national and local charities including Home-Start UK, National Youth Agency and the Chartered Institute of Housing. Diane is a senior fundraiser with a proven track record in securing monies, primarily from major trusts and foundations and the Big Lottery Fund. She has provided bespoke fundraising support, advice and strategic planning to a number of local charities.

Karen Mogan

Karen is a trained facilitator and has more than 20 years of experience in the National Health Service; training staff and implementing new policy guidelines.
