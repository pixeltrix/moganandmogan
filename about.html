About Us

Michael Mogan has more than eighteen years of experience
of fundraising at the highest level. Michael raised millions for Coventry Cathedral during his eleven year tenure and has
also raised significant sums for music, health, community,
welfare and sport charities.

Testimonials from the current Dean, former Provost and the
Chairman have kindly been provided for this site.

Karen Mogan is a trained facilitator and has more than
20 years of experience in the National Health Service;
training staff and implementing new policy guidelines.
